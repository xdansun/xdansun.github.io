Deep decarbonization of Pennsylvania's electricity sector

In 2016, the U.S. released its mid-century strategy for reducing carbon dioxide emissions by 80% of 2005 levels by 2050. Deep decarbonization will require significant changes to Pennsylvania's electricity sector. In this tool, we explore the kind of changes the Pennsylvania electricity sector must make if they are to meet previous deep decarbonizations made by U.S. The default values are set to 2015 numbers.
